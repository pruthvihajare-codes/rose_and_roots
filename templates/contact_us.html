✨ Frequently Asked Questions ✨

Everything you need to know about our products and services
How long does it take to receive my order?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days depending on your location. We also offer express delivery options for urgent orders. Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email or WhatsApp.

Do you accept custom orders?

Absolutely! We love bringing your ideas to life. Whether it's a specific color scheme, personalization, or a completely unique design, we'd be happy to discuss your custom order. Please reach out via WhatsApp or email with your requirements, and we'll provide a quote and timeline (typically 7-10 days).

What is your return policy?

We take great care in packaging every item to ensure it reaches you in perfect condition. If you receive a damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours with photos, and we'll arrange a replacement or refund. Custom orders are non-returnable unless damaged during transit.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and WhatsApp. You can track your order directly on our website by visiting your account dashboard or clicking the tracking link sent to you.

Do you offer bulk orders for events or corporate gifting?

Yes! We specialize in bulk orders for weddings, corporate events, festivals, and special occasions. We offer special discounts for bulk orders. Please contact us with your requirements, and we'll create a customized quote for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and net banking. For bulk orders, we also accept bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within India only. However, we're working on expanding our shipping capabilities. For international inquiries, please contact us directly, and we'll see if we can accommodate your order.

How should I care for my handmade craft items?

Each product comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend keeping items away from direct sunlight, moisture, and dust. For resin items, clean with a soft, dry cloth. For flower arrangements, keep in a cool, dry place away from direct heat.
